# Civics and Community Engagement Project
## Digital Literacy Initiative & Workshop

**Project Overview**
This repository contains materials and resources for a Digital Literacy Initiative designed to empower community members with essential online skills. In today's interconnected world, digital literacy is a fundamental civic responsibility. This workshop  aims to bridge the digital divide by teaching participants how to navigate the internet safely, critically evaluate online information, and build a positive digital footprint.

Our project, focuses on three high-impact areas:
* **Internet Safety and Cyber awareness:** Teaching students to identify phishing scams, securing personal data, understanding privacy settings, fake news, and financial scams to protect their families.
* **AI for Productivity and Online Earning Basics:** Demonstrating how AI tools like Gemini and ChatGPT can be used as personalized tutors and professional assistants. Also introducing students to the global digital economy.
* **Responsible Social Media Use for Students:** Responsible online etiquette, managing screen time, and fostering positive, inclusive digital communities.

**Included Files and Folders:**
* `Documentation` - Documentation Pdf include workshop details and analysis.
* `Website` - Main pages for website including logos.
* `Surveys` - Surveys pdfs for pre and post workshop assessement and QRCode(Handouts for participants to take home.) for Website.
* `Quiz` - Quizes for students to assess their progress and to answer their questions.
